ESP8266 Debugging Guide

Read bootloader message
1. Open Serial Monitor to 74880 baud rate.
2. Press the reset button on the ESP8266 chip.
..* May need to disconnect GPIO0 (pin D3 on the Wemos D1 mini) from ground
